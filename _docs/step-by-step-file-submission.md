## How to Create a Deployment in the Glider DAC? 
Documentation Link: [Deployment creation](https://ioos.github.io/glider-dac/ngdac-netcdf-file-submission-process#deployment-creation)

> **IMPORTANT:**  
> Do not create a new deployment by logging into the ftp server. You must use the process described here.

1. Request a WMO ID from [glider.dac.support@noaa.gov](mailto:glider.dac.support@noaa.gov) if your data needs to be released on the GTS.
2. Navigate to the GliderDAC providers page.
3. Click **Login** and enter your account credentials.

 >  <img width="1434" height="238" alt="step3_login" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1dfd0995-5755-412d-9f8e-36d0a64f557f" />

4. Click **Your Deployments** to register your deployment.

>   <img width="1429" height="336" alt="step4" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4004bfbb-313c-4ea3-a95c-ca6e9066b67b" />

5. Enter the glider deployment information in the registration form.

>    <img width="1430" height="392" alt="step5" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b3948dbd-1d38-498c-a753-a5a6164ebf96" />

6. Click **New Deployment** to create the deployment.

>    <img width="1429" height="276" alt="step6" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9a2493c7-625d-4d09-b15f-d5e75a29de10" />
 
7. Click the **deployment** entry name to take you to the deployment metadata page.

>   <img width="1429" height="276" alt="step7" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/da762b44-cdf0-46ef-9850-37c8044fe0a1" />

8. Specify the **Operator** on the deployment metadata page and click **Submit**.

>   <img width="1028" height="876" alt="step8" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/327e567e-7d2f-4abe-88de-b647bcc39acf" />



## How to Add Files to the Deployment Folder?
Documentation Link: [Submission of NetCDF files](https://ioos.github.io/glider-dac/ngdac-netcdf-file-submission-process#submission-of-netcdf-files)

9.  Login to the ftp website `ftp://gliders.ioos.us`.

>   <img width="766" height="125" alt="step9" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/df9fe533-d0b6-4cd6-aab7-18b2ca976ef0" />


10. List directories. Your deployment directory, the one on the providers’ page, should appear in the list.

>   <img width="766" height="99" alt="step10" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bcb3d9a9-72bf-439a-8a23-d1d272ca1b29" />


11. Change to your deployment directory.
   
   > **IMPORTANT:** This step is important to get your files submitted under the intended deployment.  

>   <img width="260" height="32" alt="step11" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ff83772c-9395-46e3-913f-37812ccd9a29" />

12. Change to your local directory with the deployment files.

>   <img width="766" height="44" alt="step12a" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9be0e564-f032-4269-acd6-856788f3b9fc" />

>   <img width="739" height="422" alt="step12b" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b4fb209c-9974-4278-8b69-c5d82203f09a" />

13. Push the files specific to your deployment.

>   <img width="373" height="454" alt="step13" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/82a0a228-df52-4e46-9004-252e1f88dc09" />

14. See your deployment files on the providers’ page.

>   <img width="622" height="541" alt="step14" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/cacd44d4-6ba9-44fc-88a6-d8784bec4bf5" />

15. Click **Complete** when the glider has been recovered or the deployment has been completed.

>   <img width="632" height="550" alt="step15" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/77ecaf71-8b01-49e1-856e-8646b0154bff" />
